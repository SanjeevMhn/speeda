Why we are different

We understand the challenges faced by business professionals looking at Asia, especially in researching on private companies and industries. Unlike public listed companies where data is freely available, details such as financials of private companies in many Asian countries must be purchased individually. Industry research are also focused on key sectors and regions only. This is what SPEEDA, a truly Asia-focused research platform with unparalleled coverage of Asian private company data and proprietary industry research reports organised by country, aims to solve. Our team of consultants also go to the extra mile to ease the research challenges faced by our users so that they can focus their time on better data-driven decision making and analysis.
